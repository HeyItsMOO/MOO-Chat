Welcome to MOO Chat. This guide takes you from sign-up to a live assistant on your site in about five minutes.

## 1. Create your account

Head to the [sign-up page](/signup) and create a free account. No credit card is required — the Free plan includes 100 AI replies per month.

## 2. Onboard your business

After signing up you'll be guided through onboarding, which creates your **tenant** (your account), your **assistant**, and your **domain allowlist** in one step. You can:

- Enter your business name and website.
- Optionally paste your website URL to **auto-fill** the knowledge base from your existing content.

## 3. Add your knowledge

The assistant only answers from what you give it. In the dashboard's **Assistant** editor, fill in:

- **Persona** — who the assistant is and how it should sound.
- **Knowledge base** — products, policies, hours, FAQs.
- **Guardrails** — rules that keep replies on-topic and compliant.

The more specific your knowledge base, the better the answers.

## 4. Make it yours

Still in the Assistant editor, set your brand colors, header, welcome message, and a few suggested questions. Use the live preview to see changes instantly.

## 5. Install on your site

Open the **Install** page in the dashboard to copy your one-line snippet:

```html
<script src="https://YOUR-APP-DOMAIN/embed.js" data-key="moo_xxxxxxxx" async></script>
```

Paste it before the closing `</body>` tag on your site. That's it — the assistant is live.

> Using WordPress or Shopify? See [Install on WordPress](/docs/install-on-wordpress) or [Install on Shopify](/docs/install-on-shopify) for the plugin-based setup.
