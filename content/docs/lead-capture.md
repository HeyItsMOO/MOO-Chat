ChatMOO turns conversations into leads with a built-in enquiry form. Here's how to set it up and work the leads you collect.

## Configure the lead form

In the **Assistant** editor, open the lead-capture section:

- **Enable** the form and set its title, intro, and button label.
- **Fields** — choose what to ask for. Keep it short: name, email, and one or two qualifying fields convert best.
- **Success message** — what the visitor sees after submitting.
- **Notify email** — where new leads are sent (defaults to the account owner).

## How it appears

The assistant offers the form at natural moments — for example when a visitor asks for a quote or wants someone to follow up. Because the form appears *after* the assistant has helped, it feels like a next step rather than a gate.

## Get notified

When a lead comes in, ChatMOO emails your **notify email** with the details and the conversation context. If email isn't configured yet, submissions are still saved to your dashboard.

## Review and export

In the dashboard:

- **Leads** — every submission, newest first, with the fields you collected.
- **Export** — download your leads as CSV to import into your CRM or spreadsheet.
- **Conversations** — read the full transcript behind each lead for context before you follow up.

## Best practices

1. Ask for the minimum you need to follow up.
2. Use a qualifying field (budget, timeframe, service) to prioritize.
3. Follow up fast — speed-to-lead is one of the biggest predictors of conversion.
